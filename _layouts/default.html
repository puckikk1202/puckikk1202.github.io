Yichen Peng

I am a research assistant professor at the Koike Lab, Institute of Science Tokyo (ex. Tokyo Institute of Technology). Currently, I visiting KLab, advised by Prof. Kris Kitani, Carnegie Mellon University. My research focuses on computer vision, human motion understanding, and multimodal generative models, with applications in skill training and human motion generation.

I received his Master and Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST) in 2021, 2024 respectively, supervised by Prof. Kazunori Miyata.(Funded by JST-SPRING) I worked closely with Prof. Haoran Xie, and Prof. Tsukasa Fukasato in Waseda University, on sketch-based 2D/3D Generation & Interface.
